About Us

History

HR eXcellency was founded in 2014 by Thato Kobisi, a seasoned HR enthusiast with a vision to provide high-quality HR-related matters to customers in the local area. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the most reputable HR consultancy business in the region, with a focus on customer satisfaction and long-term relationships.

Aims and Objectives

At HR eXcellency, our aim is to provide the best possible service to our customers, from the moment they walk in the door to the moment they leave our premises. We are committed to providing a wide selection of quality HR services at competitive prices, as well as financing options and a range of after-sales services. Our ultimate objective is to establish HR eXcellency as the go-to destination for all your HR needs.
